Domain Generalization Methods — LOBO Benchmark
All 7 methods call per_env_loss(model, batch) → includes all 4 task losses automatically.
ERM
Pool all basins, minimize mean loss
IRM
Invariant gradients across basins (cls only)
V-REx
Variance penalty on per-env losses
CORAL
Align feature covariances cross-basin
DANN
Adversarial domain discriminator + GRL
MAML
Meta-learn fast basin adaptation
PhysIRM ★
IRM on z_phys + orthogonality + physics grounding
